Quick Setup Guide
  1. Enable 2-Step Verification on your Google account
  2. Go to Google Account → Security → App Passwords
  3. Create an App Password named "SmartRefill"
  4. Copy the 16-character password — paste it in the field above
  5. Enter your Gmail address and click Save SMTP Settings
  6. Done! 🎉 Alerts will now fire automatically whenever stock runs low
